    Overview

    2024 Ford MustangDarkHorse Interior Features

    The 2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse horsepower may appear to be the star of the show, but a quick peek into this four-seat car reveals a classy interior crafted with quality materials for comfort, convenience, safety, and quiet luxury. The Mustang seats up to four people, and despite its sleek, low-profile design, the interior boasts a spacious passenger area measuring 82.8 cubic feet.
    In the front seat, enjoy a roomy 37.6 inches of headroom and 44.5 inches of legroom, while your rear-seat passengers will also experience plenty of comfort with 29 inches of legroom and 34.8 inches of headroom. While driving, enjoy an armrest and a footrest for ultimate comfort, premium carpeted floor mats with an accent stitch, and an auto-dimming frameless rearview mirror.
    The inside of your Ford Mustang Dark Horse is as thoughtfully crafted as the outside. To experience this coherent design in person, feel free to give us a call at Tom Wood Ford and schedule a viewing today.

    2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse Tech

    When you drive a 2024 Mustang Dark Horse, every little detail is at your fingertips. Calibrate the lighting with MyColor, an ambient lighting system, or turn up the music with the B&O® Sound System by Bang & Olufsen® with 12 individual speakers to immerse your passengers in sound. Regulate your temperature with heated front seats. You can even enjoy cooled front seats if you upgrade to the Dark Horse Premium top-of-the-line trim. This futuristic coupe also has internet access so that you can stay connected while on the go. Discover built-in navigation technology, a wireless charging pad, SYNC 4 system with a 13.2-inch touch screen, SiriusXM® Radio, and FordPass Connect with a 4G LTE Wi-Fi hotspot.

    2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse Specs

    All 2024 Ford Mustang trims come standard with a unitized welded steel body, aluminum vented hood, steel front fenders, and a range of features tailored toward excellent performance. Fully independent front and rear suspension produces a forgiving driving experience, and an intensive suite of safety features is standard all the way up the trim ladder. So, what makes the 2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse trim so unique?
    With its moody and menacing look, the Dark Horse has its own character. The 2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse engine, a supercharged 5-liter Coyote V-8 engine, pushes its speed and agility to the next level. This jump forward in performance comes with a price because the Dark Horse has a much higher MSRP than its counterparts for the new model year. This trim level also unlocks a TREMEC manual gearbox for its six-speed manual transmission, a strong engine, brake, and rear axle cooling system, and massive wheels that can be upgraded to an even more impressive size with certain packages
